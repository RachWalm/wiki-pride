Who Are We?

Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive and comprehensive LGBQT Wiki website that serves as a central hub of knowledge and support for the LGBQT community. We aim to foster understanding, acceptance, and celebration of diversity by providing accurate and up-to-date information on LGBQT history, culture, rights, and resources. Through collaborative efforts and community engagement, our platform will empower individuals, educate allies, and build a more informed and compassionate society. We envision a world where everyone, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity, feels valued, respected, and celebrated.
